Introduction

The Solar System consists of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, including planets, moons, asteroids, comets, and more.

Planets

There are eight major planets in the Solar System: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune.

Moons

Moons are natural satellites that orbit planets. Earth has one moon, while other planets like Jupiter have dozens.
